Emphasis-DB

During my time at General Assembly Denver, I had the exciting opportunity to work in a team of three building an automatically populating online database for the Emphasis AI project, an experimental text editor that utilizes algorithms based on the latest neuroscience theory to highlight your text based on how engaging it is to the human brain.

I was able to develop the server that interfaces with the Emphasis AI API, performing analysis on each new data entry as it comes in, either from users who wish to analyze excerpts of text they have found or written, or scraped from the Internet by the bots contained in the server code. You can take a look at the project here, and add things to the database for analysis yourself!
